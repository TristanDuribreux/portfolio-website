Brief

Quest is a company that offers music VOD
services over a range of genres.
I was charged with designing a landing
page that embodies the company
as best as possible.

Inspirations

I took inspiration from the rap culture and
music covers that feature vibrant colours and
rounded shapes that convey a feeling of
relaxation and excitement.

process

Using Figma, I began by brainstorming
and laying ideas down on the paper.
Then I made a wireframe from a user flow
a client would take on the website.

End-product

The final product has a hero section,
a review section, and a genres section
that present all kinds of music we can listen to.
