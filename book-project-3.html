Brief

Area is a company that sells jetpacks for
amateurs and professionnals. I was charged
with designing a landing page that conveys the
playfulness and fiability of the brand.

Inspirations

After doing my research about the industry
I've settled on displaying pixel-art graphics
and realistic images to create contrast and
to catch the attention of the user.

process

Using Adobe Photoshop I made monochromatic
by retouching no-copyright images. And the
graphics directly in Figma using the pen tool.

End-product

At the end, the landing page looks both playful
and modern.
