Brief

labroche is a kebab restaurant that promotes arabic
gastronomy. I was charged with designing the whole
brand's identity from its logo to the looks of the front.

Inspirations

I took inspiration from the rap culture and
music covers that feature vibrant colours and
rounded shapes that convey a feeling of
relaxation and excitement.

process

I did research about the history of the kebab.
And I noticed it was cooked with a spice called
Sumac. I thus decided to put it aside for later.

End-product

The reddish Sumac colour became
the brand's colour. I also made a menu card
and a logo with the same color scheme.
